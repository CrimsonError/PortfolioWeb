Hi! I'm CrimsonError, but you can call me Crimson.

My name is Crimson and I'm typically pretty talky and extroverted! I am an intermediate programmer and work as an advanced environmental artist. I regularly play PC games in my free time, particularly Roblox and a mix of other dystopian genres (Fallout, Half Life 2, etc.), and hanging around with my friends.

Besides just gaming and designing, I love playing the Saxophone (Alto) and listening to Jazz or Synth depending on my mood. I love meeting new people and getting friends, so please don't be afraid to reach out! If you are curious about my interests, you can reach me using the contact tab! (I don't bite :3)
